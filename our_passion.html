Our Passion

Who are we and why do we care?

For me, recycling is important because it protects the climate and new raw materials can be obtained through proper recycling. It's also about raising awareness of recycling, as people often don't know how to recycle properly... or they don't care. People are often unaware of the effects of incorrect waste separation, that's why this website is important to me, to provide an understanding of recycling.
