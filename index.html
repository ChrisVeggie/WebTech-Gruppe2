Recycle4Life

This website tends to the very important topic of recycling. We want to clarify how to properly recycle and why everyone should care about recycling. We are also going to explore how improved recycling habits can have a seriously positive impact in the grand scheme of things.

Our goal is to inspire positive change in behaivor regarding recycling rubbish. We are very lucky to have access to such a thought out recycling system in Styria, however it is often misunderstood, misused and in some extreme cases even ignored.

Our world is starving for positive change, however it does not seem like change is happening, or atleast not happening at the pace it could. We want to share our thoughts on recycling and show its purpose.

Often humans work in workflows, not thinking about or considering the possible consequences of certain actions. Once a habbit has been established, it often needs external thought provoking input to inspire change.

Its never too late to do something positive
