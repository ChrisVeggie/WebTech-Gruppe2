Recycling Process

Key steps of recycling

Here you can learn how the recycling cycle generally takes place:

RECYCLING PROCESS

STEP 1:The first Step is theCOLLECTION: Collection methods vary from municipality to municipality. After collection, recyclables must be sent to a materials recovery facility. Using a recycling facility, they are sorted and processed into marketable goods. They can then be sold to processing companies.
STEP 2:Here the recyclable materials are further PROCESSED. After cleaning and sorting, the recyclables are processed in order to extract the raw materials that can then be used for the production of of products with recycled content can be used.
STEP 3:The third third step COMPLETES the recycling loop. This step involves the purchase of recycled products. It should be added that more and more of today's products are made with fully or partially recycled material.
