Why you should care

Plastic has conquered the world. It spreads far and wide with no end or alleviation in sight, with consequences becoming more prevalent and noticable, every day. As the question might arise why recycling is important, we would like to provoke a thoughtful impulse by showing off some of its consequences if done wrong or ignored. We hope to inspire whoever to feel like they are making a difference, however small it might be in the grand scheme of things.
