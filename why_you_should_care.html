Why you should care

Plastic and other modern every day materials have conquered the world. It spreads far and wide with no end or alleviation in sight, with consequences becoming more prevalent and noticable, every day. As the question might arise why recycling is important, we would like to provoke a thoughtful impulse by showing off some of its consequences if done wrong or ignored. We hope to inspire whoever to feel like they are making a difference, however small it might be in the grand scheme of things.

Reasons why you should care:

Recycling saves energy

Recycling reduces landfill

Recycling conserves resources

Recycling reduces the negative impact on the environment

Fewer harmful substances enter the environment

Stress relief for local Wildlife

A big issue regarding no proper recycling is that rubbish always finds a way into every ecosystem on our planet and spread into every corner, no matter how far away it might seem. Approximately 150 million Tons of plastic are occupying space in our ocean by now. Each year we add 5 to 13 million tons of plastic to our oceans.
